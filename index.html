About Me

I am an agile learner and software developer with 10 + years of professional experience leading multi-million dollar projects from ideation to delivering across diverse industries including telecommunications, manufacturing, and insurance. Eager to leverage excellent communication, strong relationship management skills, proven problem solving strategies, and technical expertise to a software team.

Life as a Developer

I first started doing the free online learnings and got hoocked immediately on building web pages and applications. My first taught projects were in Python and then I eventually went on to learn React. Ever since I have been writing code in my spare time and brainstorming ideas for apps. I currently host gaming servers via discord and have written a few apps to handle the traffic.
